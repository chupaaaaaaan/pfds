Exercise 10.5 (b)

The costs of empty, isEmpty, head are all obviously \(O(1)\). snoc and tail call checkQ, and checkQ calls PrimQ.snoc and checkF. As forcing $rev in checkF takes \(O(1)\) amortized time and PrimQ.head and PrimQ.tail take \(O(1)\) worst-case time, checkF takes \(O(1)\) amortized time in total. PrimQ.snoc also costs \(O(1)\), checkQ and therefore snoc and tail take \(O(1)\) amortized time.
